Twitter-Timewarp

The on hand web-application provides a time-depending network visualization of the distribution of a user-specified search-term used as #hashtag and its related #hashtags on Twitter. In short it visualizes the in- and decrease of the frequency over time (evolving "buzzword" importance over time). Supporting technologies for the development of this prototype are Python2.7 [Flask, Pattern] in interplay with Redis and 3D.js.
